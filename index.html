About EduConnect

EduConnect is a platform that connects university students who can provide free education or tutoring with underprivileged individuals in their vicinity. Our mission is to make quality education accessible to all, regardless of economic background.

Key Features

  • 📍 Geolocation-based matching
  • 📚 Subject-specific tutoring
  • 💬 Secure communication
  • 📅 Easy scheduling
  • 🎓 Volunteer recognition
  • 📱 Mobile-friendly platform
